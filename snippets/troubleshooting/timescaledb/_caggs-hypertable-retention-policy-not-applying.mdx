### Hypertable retention policy isn't applying to continuous aggregates

**APIs**:
- [`add_retention_policy()`][add-retention-policy]

A retention policy set on a {HYPERTABLE} does not apply to any {CAGG}s made from the {HYPERTABLE}.
This allows you to set different retention periods for raw and summarized data. To apply a
retention policy to a {CAGG}, set the policy on the {CAGG} itself.
